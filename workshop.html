    • WORKSHOP

    Come join us on our workshops and learn how to make your own hand-made biological soap.

    What will you learn?

    • Introduction on the process.
    • Aromatherapy
    • Combination of the essential oils fit best the soap you want to make.
    • Duration: Aprox.3 hours

    Soap will be made through the so called cold process. What does it mean? It means that the soap that you will produce will need a month to cure before it can be used. You will have plenty of time to ask question and be creative with the ingredients and colors. At the end of the workshop, each participant will have approximately 250gr of handmade soap that they will have created themselves.
